Privacy Policy

How we handle your data

Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights concerning your data while using our AI Idea Generator website.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information, such as your name, email, or other identifying data. We do not use cookies or any tracking technologies for our own purposes.

The only information collected is the prompt you type into the generator. This prompt is sent to a third-party AI service to generate content ideas and is not stored by us.

Third-Party Services

This website relies on two main third-party services:

  • Gemini API: We use the Gemini API to power the AI idea generation functionality. The prompts you enter are sent to this service for processing.
  • Google AdSense: We use Google AdSense to serve ads on our site. AdSense may collect information through cookies to provide personalized advertising. You can learn more about AdSense and how to opt out of personalized ads by visiting their privacy policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, you can contact us through our Contact Us page.
